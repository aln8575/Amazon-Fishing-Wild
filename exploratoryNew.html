What is the Exploratory?

The Exploratory is a fishing expedition designed to discover new areas in the Amazon, in remote environments where fishing pressure is virtually non-existent. Each trip offers a unique opportunity to access waters that are not part of traditional operations.

Request Availability View the Journey

What makes the Exploratory different?

The Exploratory is the origin of all our operations. For over 27 years, Wellington has developed this system to identify new areas with potential, evaluate them, and over time transform them into established fishing experiences. Each expedition is unique and takes place in a new area, meaning the Exploratory is constantly evolving and is never repeated in the same way. This approach not only allows us to discover new waters, but also helps maintain a natural balance in the ecosystem, avoiding overfishing and ensuring consistent fishing experiences over time.

Where does this expedition take place?

The current expedition takes place on the Jari River, within a protected reserve in Brazil where we are authorized to operate. This is a remote region with controlled access, allowing its natural conditions to remain intact.

What is the camp like?

The camp is designed to provide a comfortable and well-organized base, even in the most remote areas of the Amazon. It follows a safari-style structure, allowing us to establish operations in hard-to-reach locations while maintaining a consistent standard in every expedition.

Accommodations are private tent units built on elevated wooden platforms and placed under natural tree shade. Each angler has an individual unit equipped with comfortable beds, ventilation, electricity, and a private bathroom.

What is the fishing experience like?

Fishing in the Exploratory takes place in environments where fish behavior remains natural, without constant fishing pressure. Each day adapts to the river's conditions, exploring different structures and areas.

Fishing is done from specialized boats, with two anglers per boat and an experienced guide with knowledge of the area.

In addition, each boat is equipped with Starlink internet, allowing communication and connectivity during the expedition.
