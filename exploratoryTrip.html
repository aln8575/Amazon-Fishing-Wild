How our exploratory trip works

A Journey into a Treasure Trove of Nature’s Wonders

Our exploratory trips are designed for avid fishermen with a passion for exploration, adventure and discovery.

These expeditions into the Amazon’s heart are tailored to fishermen whose ultimate goal is to venture where fish have never before seen an angler, or even a lure. We seek out the most untrammeled and untraveled regions of the Amazon Rainforest. If you yearn to be the pioneering angler, casting your lines into untouched waters, then this is the trip for you. Our quests may lead us to seek out giant peacock bass, fanged payara, massive wolfish, acrobatic bicuda and a full range of the Amazon’s renowned giant catfish. More often than not, we are left still wondering about what other unknown species may be lurking in these wild waters, driving our passion for exploration. Group size on our exploratory trips is limited to 8 anglers.

Portable enough to go anywhere a boat or airplane can get to, it's flexible enough to provide an operating base in any terrain. We've turned exploratory camping (or maybe we should say "glamping") into an artform. Wellington Melo’s regularly scheduled Fishing Wild trips all boast great food, quality wines and an open bar. So does our exploratory safari camp! Our regularly scheduled trips offer high quality rods and reels, comfortable beds, and daily laundry service. So does our camp! In fact, except for air-conditioning (we have fans) and private bathrooms (the camp provides several bathrooms with flush toilets and pump-driven showers), there isn't a lot missing. Frankly, if you've ever gone camping, this is about as good as it gets. We come fully supplied with generators, pumps, pipes and wiring, so complete electrical and plumbing systems are put in place on our arrival in a region. This lets us implement an array of refrigerators, freezers and all of the infrastructure necessary to ensure your comfort and security in the middle of Nature’s most spectacular wilderness.

ITINERARY

Itinerary / General Information

Departure & Travel - Depending on your departure city, leave home in time to connect with a late afternoon International flight from Miami to Manaus Brazil.

ITINERARY MULTI-SPECIES VARIETY TRIP

- - Transfer day & Day 1 - Arrive Manaus, Brazil. After clearing customs you will be met by our agent and transferred to our Manaus Hotel The next morning, you will be transferred to the airport to board a chartered aircraft to the pristine river we will be exploring.- Upon arrival, relax, enjoy a meal and get your gear ready for the extraordinary fishing waters you will explore.
- Day 2 – Through Day 7 - Six full days of fishing. Your angling day begins with breakfast at 6:00 AM. Your guide will be ready to depart when you are. Return to camp by dusk for appetizers, drinks and bragging, followed by a delicious dinner. Jungle hikes, spotlighting trips and bird watching can be arranged. We ask that you give us all a report on what you experienced each day, so that we can compile the fishing data for the region.
- Travel day & Return – Prepare for your charter flight back to Manaus. Depending on distance from the river, arrival times will vary. Generally, you will be provided with a day room at the Hotel and you will time for some sightseeing and relaxing. Late evening, transfer to airport. Depart via International flight for home
