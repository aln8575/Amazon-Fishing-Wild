Who We Are

Amazon Fishing Wild is an American company specialized in sport fishing trips in the Amazon. Backed by more than 27 years of experience under Wellington Melo’s professional trajectory, we have refined every operational, logistical and human detail through decades of expeditions.

The experience is the same.

The operational capacity is the same.

The commitment is the same.

We are vertically integrated — we design, sell and operate every expedition. There are no intermediaries between what we promise and what we deliver.

Why Fish With Us

Because we don’t just sell a trip — we operate and live the experience. Every route, every camp detail, and every logistical decision is overseen by professionals who truly know the Amazon from the inside.

While you focus on the perfect cast and the moment of the strike, everything else is in expert hands.
