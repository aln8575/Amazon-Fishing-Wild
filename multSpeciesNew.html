What is the Multi-Species?

The Multi-Species is an experience created for anglers who want to experience the Amazon in a complete, comfortable, and immersive way.

Here, comfort does not separate you from the jungle. The river, the sounds of nature, and the feeling of being surrounded by the Amazon become part of every moment of the experience.

The combination of lodge accommodations, fishing diversity, and different river structures creates dynamic days where each day can feel completely different from the last.

More than an operation focused on a single species, the Multi-Species was designed for anglers seeking to experience the Amazon in all its diversity.

Remote Amazon river systems selected for diversity.

Where does this operation take place?

The Multi-Species operates in remote Amazon river systems specifically selected for their natural structure and fishing diversity.

These environments include rocky formations, fast-water sections, deep channels, and multiple current systems, creating ideal conditions for both surface and deep-water species.

The regions are highly isolated from urban centers and large communities, helping preserve a quiet environment fully connected to the Amazon rainforest.

The diversity of river structure is exactly what allows this operation to provide multiple fishing techniques and experiences within the same trip.

Comfort without disconnecting from the jungle.

How is the lodge experience?

The lodge was designed to provide real comfort without disconnecting guests from the Amazon itself.

Private wooden cabins positioned directly in front of the river are interconnected by elevated walkways that naturally move through the vegetation and connect every area of the operation.

The structure integrates with the jungle rather than isolating itself from it. From the moment guests open the door to their room, the sounds of the river, birds, and rainforest become part of the experience.

Each accommodation includes air conditioning, hot water, comfortable beds, storage space, and private bathrooms while maintaining the authentic feeling of being immersed in nature.

Riverfront atmosphere

A place to relax after every fishing day.

The common areas were designed for relaxation and social interaction after each fishing day. The main dining area and kitchen connect to a riverfront deck where guests gather in the late afternoon for appetizers, drinks, and conversation surrounded by the Amazon landscape.

At night, torch-lit walkways create a quiet and immersive atmosphere where the only sounds come from the river and jungle itself.

The Multi-Species was created to allow guests to experience the Amazon comfortably while remaining fully connected to the environment around them.

Dynamic, varied, and adaptable throughout the week.

How is the fishing experience?

The Multi-Species fishing experience was designed to remain dynamic, varied, and adaptable throughout the week.

Unlike operations focused on a single technique or species, each fishing day can develop differently depending on angler preference and river conditions.

Anglers may choose to focus on surface species, deep-water fish, or combine multiple techniques throughout the day. Based on those preferences, guides adapt the experience and navigate to different structures and river sections according to the desired objective.

Fishing is done from boats specifically designed for this type of operation, offering both stability and comfort throughout the day. Each boat accommodates two anglers and one guide, and includes swivel seats with back support for greater comfort during long fishing sessions.

Guides possess extensive knowledge of the area and help adapt each fishing day according to river conditions and guest interests.

The result is an active and immersive fishing experience where every day presents new possibilities within the same Amazon ecosystem.
