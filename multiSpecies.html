Multi Species Trip

Surrounded by spectacular primavel forest, the Rio Guariba is home to a broad swath of the Amazon's incredible biodiversity.

About the Guariba River

In this unique and exclusive fishery, seekers of Nature's untrammeled essence find themselves immersed in a true paradise.
The rio Guariba is the pinnacle of freshwater fishing experiences. With its unparalleled variety, this glorious river provides anglers with the opportunity to catch a lifetime's worth of sought after species - all in one trip. Here, far from the trappings of civilization, sport fishermen can experience the heart-pounding excitement of hand-to-hand combat with the Amazon's incredible gamefish.
Welcome to the "Land of the Giants"!

Comfort in the middle of the jungle

Your Rio Guariba fishing experience is complemented by Guariba Lodge's unexpected "Jungle luxury. Comfortable private cabins or spacious Couple's Suites provide all of the comforts of home.

Accommodations

First class service makes your comfort complete. All of your needs are carefully attended to by our team of hospitality professionals.
