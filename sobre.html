Hi, I'm Wellington Mello.

For over 27 years, I've been living for this, exploring the hidden corners of the Amazon, chasing trophy fish, and creating unforgettable experiences. I'm passionate about sharing my love for these waters and guiding anglers to their own fishing success.

I've spent years mastering the art of fishing in the Amazon, from understanding how to fish the most challenging rapids to exploring new fisheries in the most remote locations.

My expertise has led to numerous IGFA world records being caught on my trips. I'm dedicated to providing high-quality service and unforgettable experiences for every angler.

I served as a manager and representative for a leading American fishing company for over 20 years in Brazil, where I gained valuable experience and built a strong reputation for delivering exceptional trips.

Now, I'm excited to offer my expertise and passion through my own company, providing the same exceptional service and creating even more unique and unforgettable adventures.
