My Journey in the Amazon

My passion for fishing began deep in the Amazon, where understanding the river, the seasons, and the fish is everything. Over the years, I mastered techniques for challenging rapids, hidden lagoons, and unexplored waters.

I spent more than 20 years as a manager and representative for a major American fishing company in Brazil, building a solid reputation for delivering world-class fishing experiences.

Today, through Amazon Fishing Wild, I offer anglers exclusive access to remote fisheries, professional guidance, and trips designed for true adventure seekers.
